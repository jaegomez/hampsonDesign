Project Management

Most Sub-Contractors that we use have worked with Hampson Design Construction for over a decade. We specialize in Project Management of Renovation which means that Ian Hampson collaborates and communicates with the client on all the design elements of the project from the beginning till the end, in addition to handling the daily scheduling and management of all site Sub-Contractors

Design

Ian Hampson works with clients on a full-scale Design of remodels, that consists of multiple layouts, configuration, selection of flooring, cabinetry, counter-tops, tile and stone, plumbing fixtures, hardware, doors, paint, audio and visual technology, or in which ever way the clients needs can be surpassed..

Consultations

If they simply need inspiration or an opinion about the direction they are headed we recommend a Consultation.However some clients have an established style for their renovations and don't need a full design or a project management service. Ian consults on remodels like this to assist in narrowing the selection of finishes from flooring, cabinetry, counter tops, tile and stone, lighting, hardware, fixtures and paint to ensure uniformity.
